Our Journey & Purpose

Our History

Founded on the principles of holistic education, Evergreen Academy started with just 50 students in a small historic building. Today, we serve over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds, maintaining the same intimate, student-centered approach that defined our early years.

Our Mission

"To empower students to reach their full potential through a rigorous academic curriculum, a supportive community, and a commitment to global citizenship and ethical leadership."

Our Vision

To be a global leader in innovative education, recognized for producing graduates who are not only academically exceptional but also compassionate problem-solvers.
