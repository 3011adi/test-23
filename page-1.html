A Message from Dr. Elizabeth Sterling

At Evergreen Academy, we believe that every child possesses a unique spark of potential. Our mission is to nurture that spark into a flame of curiosity, creativity, and leadership.

Our holistic approach to education combines rigorous academic standards with a vibrant arts program and competitive athletics, ensuring our graduates are well-rounded individuals ready to navigate an ever-changing global landscape.

I invite you to explore our campus, meet our dedicated faculty, and discover why Evergreen is more than just a school—it's a community of lifelong learners.

Dr. Elizabeth Sterling

Head of School, PhD in Educational Leadership
