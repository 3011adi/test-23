Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our admissions process.

What is the application deadline?

+
Applications for the main intake close on March 31st each year. However, we do accept rolling applications if seats are available in specific grades.

Is there an age requirement for Grade 1?

+
Students must be 6 years old by September 1st of the year they wish to enroll in Grade 1.

How long does the assessment process take?

+
The assessment typically takes 2-3 hours and results are shared with parents within 5 working days.
